about me

Full stack developer and designer dedicated to crafting engaging digital experiences. Leveraging creative design sensibilities and proficient coding skills to create user-centric web solutions that blend aesthetics and functionality seamlessly.

A designer turned developer, I specialize in translating research insights into thoughtful solutions, seamlessly aligning user needs, business objectives, and technical constraints.

I am experienced in developing and maintaining web applications using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, Node.js, and PostgreSQL, in an AGILE environment. This comprehensive skill set empowers me to craft elegant websites and sophisticated, database-driven applications with confidence.

Collaboration and communication are at the heart of my creative process. Through effective liaison with clients and stakeholders, I excel in conveying concepts, iterating based on feedback, and thriving in cross-functional teams.
My natural problem-solving mindset enables me to navigate challenges encountered in both design and development journeys. I possess a keen eye for identifying and resolving issues, including those related to browser compatibility, performance optimization, and code debugging.

Beyond my professional pursuits, I find joy in making things interactive through creative coding and physical computing and am always curious to learn more when it comes to new technologies.
